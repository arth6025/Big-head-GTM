# Workshop Plan: "SEO is Dead for DTC. Here's What's Replacing It."

*A Big Head / ShopOS acquisition + activation event for Shopify SEO agencies*
*Format reference: AIBoomi "SEO → AEO" roundtable, Bengaluru*
*Owner: PMM*

---

## Concept

A curated, closed-room roundtable for Shopify SEO agency owners and account leads. Not a webinar. Not a product demo. A practitioner conversation about the shift from traditional SEO to AI visibility — with Big Head as the host and the authority in the room.

The goal is not to pitch. The goal is to be the most useful 2 hours any agency has spent on this topic — and have every attendee leave with:
1. A live AI visibility audit of one of their client stores (run in the room)
2. A copy of the AEO playbook for Shopify agencies
3. A reason to trial the agency dashboard

---

## Title Options

1. **"SEO is Dead for DTC. Here's What's Replacing It."** *(provocative, high open rate)*
2. **"Your DTC Clients Are Invisible on AI Search. Here's the Fix."** *(problem-first, agency POV)*
3. **"AEO for Shopify: The New Service Your Agency Should Be Selling"** *(revenue angle, agency owner)*

Recommended: Option 1 as the event title, Option 3 as the subtitle.

## Target Attendees

**Primary:** Shopify-focused SEO agency owners and account directors (managing 5–30 DTC brand clients each)

**Secondary:** Freelance Shopify growth consultants, Klaviyo/Postscript partner agencies

**Audience size:** 15–25 people max. Closed, curated. No general marketers. Application or invite-only to create scarcity and signal quality.

**Ideal cities for first run:** Bangalore · Mumbai · Delhi · London · New York · Sydney (where Shopify agency density is highest)

## Format

| Block | Duration | What Happens |
|---|---|---|
| Welcome + framing | 15 min | Host sets the scene: the SEO → AEO shift in 5 data points. No slides beyond slide 1. |
| Live audit demo | 20 min | Run a real `/audit` on a volunteer's client store. Live. Everyone sees the gap in real time. |
| Roundtable: what's working | 40 min | Facilitated discussion — what are agencies seeing? What have they tried? What's failing? |
| The AEO playbook for agencies | 25 min | Big Head walks through the 5-step playbook. Practical. No theory. |
| Q&A + open floor | 15 min | Unstructured. Best conversations happen here. |
| Networking | 30 min | Drinks. Every attendee gets the playbook PDF and a 30-day agency dashboard trial code. |

**Total:** ~2.5 hours

---

## The Live Audit Moment (Most Important Part)

Before the event, ask each attendee to nominate one client store URL. During the event, pick 2–3 of these and run `/audit` live on the screen.

What the room sees:
- Visibility score appearing in under 5 minutes
- The exact prompts their client's customers are typing into ChatGPT
- Which competitors are being cited (and why)
- The gap — expressed as missed citations per week, not as an abstract score

This is the "holy sh*t" moment that converts the room. Seeing a real client store score 14/100 while a competitor scores 71/100 is visceral. No pitch needed after that.

---

## The Playbook (What Attendees Take Away)

A printed + digital "AEO for Shopify Agencies" playbook covering:

1. **How to audit any client store** — the 5-minute `/audit` walk-through
2. **How to pitch AI visibility to clients** — the 3-slide deck insert (fits into any QBR)
3. **How to price it** — three packaging options (audit-only, audit + setup, full managed AEO)
4. **What to measure** — the 4 metrics clients care about (citation volume, share of voice, prompt coverage, competitor gap)
5. **How to deliver it** — the agency dashboard walk-through

This playbook is the lead magnet. Gated version available at bighead.shopos.ai/agency-playbook. Physical version given to event attendees only.

## Acquisition + Activation Loop

```
Agency attends workshop
    → runs live audit on client store (activation in the room)
    → leaves with playbook + 30-day agency dashboard trial
    → pitches AI visibility to 1-2 clients within 2 weeks (playbook makes this easy)
    → client sees first citation
    → agency converts to paid agency tier
    → agency runs audits on 10+ more clients (referral multiplier)
```

Each workshop attendee is a potential 10–20x revenue multiplier — every client they convert is a new Big Head subscription.

## Event Logistics

**Platform:** Luma (registration, waitlist, reminders, post-event follow-up)

**Format options:**
- In-person (preferred for first 3 events — intimacy drives trust)
- Online roundtable via Zoom/Hopin for scaled runs (max 30 people, breakout rooms for small group discussions)

**Venue:** Co-working spaces, agency offices, Shopify's local offices (if partnership is in place). Avoid hotel conference rooms — too corporate.

**Refreshments:** Yes. The networking block is 30% of the value. Make it comfortable.

**Recording:** No. Closed-room creates candour and FOMO. "You had to be there" drives waitlist signups for future events.
