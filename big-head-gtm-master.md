## What Big Head Does

Big Head makes sure DTC brands show up when shoppers ask AI what to buy.

It audits your Shopify store, identifies which prompts your customers are typing into ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude, scores your current visibility across all four engines, then automatically generates and publishes citable content — tracking every citation in a unified dashboard.

**Entry:** Free. No credit card. Run `/audit` — first score in under 5 minutes.
**Ongoing:** 20+ AI-ready pages published weekly, fully automated.

## PLG Activities by Stage

### ACQUIRE

**1. `/audit` — The Core Hook**
User hits `/audit`, enters their store URL. No email required to start. Score is shown immediately ("Your AI Visibility Score: 23/100 — you're mostly invisible").

Immediately after the score is shown, a **playbook lead magnet** is surfaced:
> "Want the full playbook to fix this? We've mapped the exact steps for brands in your category."
> [Get the free playbook → enter email]

Email is captured at peak intent — after they've seen the gap, before they leave. Playbook is emailed instantly. This is the top-of-funnel email capture moment.

**2. Shareable Score Card**
Every audit generates a branded, one-click shareable card showing visibility scores per engine and a competitor gap line. High scores get shared as bragging rights. Low scores get shared as "look at the problem I'm solving." Both generate inbound.

**3. "Is My Competitor Being Cited?" Free Tool**
Ungated. Enter a category + a competitor name → Big Head checks live across ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini → shows citation rate. CTA: "Check your own brand." Competitor FOMO is the strongest emotional driver in this category.

**4. AI Visibility Benchmark Report** *(Lead Magnet — see `lead-magnet-benchmark-report.md`)*
Quarterly report auditing the top Shopify brands by category. Who's winning AI search in beauty, apparel, wellness, home, food. PR hook + outbound weapon + SEO content. Gate: email only, with top 10 rankings visible ungated.
> *(See also: `pmm-task-leaderboard.md` — PMM task to turn this into a social media release)*

**5. Shopify App Store Listing**
Free install. Shopify passes store metadata automatically — no URL entry needed. Category: Marketing → SEO & Traffic. The highest-intent distribution channel for this audience.

**6. Agency Prospecting Angle** *(Agency-specific)*
For agencies: run `/audit` on a prospect's store before the pitch meeting. Walk in with live data. "We already checked — here's where you stand in AI search." Position: the audit is a prospecting weapon, not just self-diagnosis.

**7. SEO/GEO Experts Contribution Wall**
A public community resource where Shopify SEO and GEO practitioners share prompt wins, content formats that get cited, technical tips, and before/after case studies. Owned by Big Head, open to the DTC community.
Every contribution has a "verify this on your store →" CTA that routes back to `/audit`.
Positions Big Head as the community hub for Shopify AI visibility — not just a tool.
> *(See `community-contribution-wall.md` for full concept and build path)*

### ACTIVATE

**8. Sign Up → Publish First 3 Pages**
After seeing their audit score, user signs up (email + store confirmation). Immediately, Big Head auto-generates and queues the first 3 AI-ready pages for their highest-opportunity prompts. User sees a one-click approval screen: "Here's what we'll publish — approve to go live." Single click to activate. No content briefing, no agency, no waiting.

**9. DNS + Google Ads Connection Nudge**
After first pages are published, Big Head surfaces three easy-win nudges in sequence:

| Nudge | What it unlocks |
|---|---|
| Connect your DNS | Big Head publishes AI-ready pages to your domain (not a subdomain) — citations carry more authority |
| Connect Google Ads | See which cited prompts are also driving paid traffic — eliminate overlap, find gaps |
| Unlock your first easy win | One high-confidence prompt Big Head identifies as "citeable within 72 hours" for your store — published and tracked automatically |

Each nudge is presented as an unlock, not a setup task. One action = one visible result.

**10. Empty State as a Teaching Tool**
New users with no citation data yet see: live competitor citations in their category, a progress bar ("3 pages published → awaiting index → first citation estimated in 3–5 days"), and an estimated first citation date. No blank screens.

**11. First Citation Notification (email)**
The moment a brand is first cited in any AI engine response, an automated email fires.
> *(See `email-copies.md` — Email 1 for the full drafted copy)*
This is the highest-leverage conversion moment in the entire funnel. Free → paid conversion rate peaks here.

### RETAIN

**12. Weekly AI Visibility Digest** *(Paid plan feature — PLG retention mechanic)*
Every Monday: visibility score vs. last week, new citations earned, competitor movement, top missed prompt, one recommended action.

> **[PM TASK]** This is a paid-tier PLG feature — not a free drip email. It creates a weekly re-acquisition moment 52x per year. The digest surfaces upgrade moments organically (competitor gaining = urgency; missed prompt = gap = upsell). Requires data pipelines for competitor tracking and personalization by segment (brand vs. agency view).
> *Full product brief in `pm-task-weekly-digest.md`*

**13. Citation Milestone Celebrations**
In-app + email at: first citation, 10 citations, 50 citations, first citation for a competitive prompt. Each milestone triggers a shareable graphic (feeds back into acquisition) and a timed upgrade suggestion.

**14. Public Citation Leaderboard** *(opt-in)*
Top-cited Shopify brands by category, updated weekly. Brands opt in for bragging rights.

> **[PMM TASK]** The leaderboard launch across the top 100 Shopify brands globally is a social media moment — not just a product feature. Requires coordinating data/eng to run audits at scale, a visual design sprint for shareable 1:1 and 16:9 graphics, and a pre-release outreach to top-ranked brands so they're ready to amplify.
> *Full campaign brief in `pmm-task-leaderboard.md`*

### REFER

**15. "Powered by Big Head" Footer**
Free-plan published pages carry a subtle attribution link. Removable on paid plans — built-in upgrade incentive. Generates passive inbound from merchants who see competitor pages and click through.

**16. Invite-a-Brand Referral Program**
Trigger: right after first citation (peak excitement). Reward: both parties get 30 extra AI-ready pages. Pre-written share copy: "Just got my Shopify store cited on ChatGPT for [query]. Used this free tool — it audits your AI visibility in 5 minutes: [link]"

**17. Agency Dashboard (Revenue Multiplier)**
First-class product tier for agencies. White-label audit reports, multi-brand dashboard, bulk prompt targeting, 20% recurring commission on converted clients. One agency with 20 clients = 20 potential subscriptions with zero additional sales effort.
