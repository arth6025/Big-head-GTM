## FAQ

### Do I need my client's permission to run an audit?
No. A Big Head audit uses the store's public-facing URL — the same information any shopper or competitor can see. You can audit any Shopify store as a prospecting exercise before a pitch, no client permission needed.

### What do I actually hand clients? Is there a deliverable?
Yes. Big Head generates a white-label AI visibility report: current visibility score, the prompts their shoppers are using, competitor citation gaps, and a recommended content roadmap. Export it under your agency brand. It's a complete deliverable for a discovery engagement or a monthly retainer review.

### Can I manage all my clients from one login?
Yes. The agency dashboard lets you view every client's visibility score, citation growth, and content output in one place. No juggling separate accounts.

### What if my clients are already paying for an SEO retainer? Won't this cannibalize that?
No. SEO optimizes for Google rankings. Big Head optimizes for AI citations. They're separate channels with separate ranking logic. Your clients need both. Big Head adds a new channel to your service offering — it doesn't replace what you're already doing.

### How does the 20% commission actually work?
When a brand you manage or refer upgrades to a paid Big Head plan, you earn 20% of their monthly subscription — every month, automatically, for as long as they remain a customer.
