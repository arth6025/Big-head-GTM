## FAQ

### What exactly is an AI citation?
When a shopper asks ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, or Claude for a recommendation, the AI names specific brands in its answer. That's a citation. Unlike Google rankings, citations aren't based on keywords — they're based on whether AI engines have seen authoritative, well-structured content about your brand. Big Head creates that content for you.

### Do I need to know anything about AI or SEO to use this?
No. Paste your Shopify store URL. Big Head does the rest. The audit, the content generation, the publishing — all automated. If you can run a Shopify store, you can use Big Head.

### Will the content Big Head publishes match my brand voice?
Big Head generates structured, authoritative content designed to be cited by AI engines. It's not generic filler. During setup, you give Big Head your brand context — tone, category, differentiators. The output is specific to your brand. [PLACEHOLDER: Add note about brand voice controls once feature is available]

### How is this different from SEO?
SEO optimizes your content to rank in Google's search results. Big Head optimizes your content to be cited in AI responses. The underlying logic is different: AI engines look for authoritative, structured, trustworthy content — not keyword density. You need both channels now. Most brands have SEO covered; almost none have AI visibility covered.

### How long until I see citations?
You'll see your audit and visibility score in under 5 minutes. After Big Head starts publishing AI-ready content, citation tracking begins immediately. How quickly citations appear depends on how active your category is in AI search — most brands see their first citations within 7–14 days of going live.
